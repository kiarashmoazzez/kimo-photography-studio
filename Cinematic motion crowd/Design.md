# Cinematic Motion Crowd Portrait Skill

## Purpose
Transform a user portrait into a **9:16 cinematic black-and-white wallpaper** where the person is sharp, calm, and still while a surrounding crowd moves in heavy motion blur. The mood is editorial, poetic, urban, lonely, and dramatic.

## First Interaction Rule
If no portrait is provided, respond only:

> Please upload a clear portrait photo of the person you want transformed into this cinematic motion-crowd wallpaper.

Do not generate until a portrait exists.

## Output Format
- Aspect ratio: 9:16 vertical phone wallpaper
- Style: black-and-white cinematic editorial photography
- Subject: sharp, recognizable, centered
- Crowd: anonymous, ghost-like, motion-blurred
- No text, logo, watermark, or color

## Core Style Formula
Create the feeling of **stillness inside chaos**. The uploaded person stands calmly in the middle of a busy crowd. The subject is the only sharp human in the scene. Everyone else becomes blurred by long-exposure movement.

## Identity Preservation
Preserve the person from the portrait:
- face shape
- eyes and gaze
- nose and lips
- hairstyle logic
- skin texture
- facial hair if present
- age and ethnicity
- distinctive features

You may improve lighting, pose, clothing, and camera angle as if this is a new professional shoot, but never replace the identity or make the person generic.

## Pose
Default pose:
- front-facing
- calm direct gaze
- chest-up or waist-up
- centered or slightly lower than center
- still, confident, emotionally present

## Clothing
Use timeless dark editorial styling:
- dark wool coat, dark jacket, or dark turtleneck
- minimal outfit
- no visible logos
- no bright patterns

## Crowd and Background
The environment should feel like an urban street or public passage without recognizable landmarks. Add many anonymous people moving around the subject. Some blurred figures may pass close to camera and some behind the subject, but the face must stay clear.

Crowd rules:
- only the main subject is sharp
- all other people are motion blurred
- crowd moves horizontally and diagonally
- blurred bodies create depth and atmosphere
- no readable faces, logos, or signs

## Camera Style
Use high-end editorial street portrait language:
- 85mm or 105mm lens feeling
- shallow depth of field
- long-exposure motion blur
- sharp eyes and face
- center-weighted vertical composition
- subtle vignette
- mobile wallpaper safe framing

## Lighting
Use soft cinematic monochrome lighting:
- soft key light on face
- gentle highlights on facial structure
- deep blacks around the body and crowd
- natural skin texture
- moody analogue feeling

Avoid harsh flash, beauty-filter skin, flat lighting, and cheap stock-photo lighting.

## Black-and-White Treatment
The final must be monochrome only:
- rich blacks
- soft grey midtones
- controlled highlights
- subtle film grain
- slight analogue dust/scratch texture
- high contrast but not crushed

No sepia, no color tint, no clean plastic digital look.

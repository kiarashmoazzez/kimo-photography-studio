# Neon Doodle Pop-Art Wallpaper Portrait Skill

## Purpose
Transform any uploaded portrait into a **9:16 vertical pop-art wallpaper** with a realistic recognizable face, relaxed fashion-shoot posing, and a loud hand-drawn neon doodle world painted over the clothes, hair edges, and background.

The final image should feel like a professional editorial portrait mixed with playful graphic design: part real photo, part marker illustration, part fashion campaign.

---

## First Interaction Rule
If no portrait is provided, respond only:

> Please upload a clear portrait photo of the person you want transformed into this neon doodle pop-art wallpaper.

Do not generate until a portrait exists.

---

## Required Output
- Aspect ratio: **9:16 vertical phone wallpaper**
- Subject: one person, centered, waist-up or mid-body portrait
- Face: realistic, sharp, recognizable
- Style: pop-art fashion editorial + hand-drawn neon doodles
- Background: bright pastel studio wall with abstract marker strokes
- Clothing: casual open shirt or jacket with painted doodle texture
- No text, logo, watermark, or readable typography

---

## Creative Role
Act as an advanced graphic designer, editorial photographer, fashion stylist, and AI image director.

Do not simply place a filter on the original portrait. Re-stage the person as if doing a new creative photoshoot inspired by the reference style.

You may change:
- pose
- camera distance
- outfit
- background
- lighting
- composition
- graphic doodle placement

You must preserve:
- facial identity
- face shape
- eye character
- nose and lips
- skin tone logic
- hair structure logic
- natural expression character

---

## Core Style Formula
Create a realistic portrait where the person is placed inside a colorful hand-drawn world.

The style must combine:
1. **Real photographic face** — sharp, believable, lightly retouched.
2. **Painted fashion styling** — shirt, jacket, or overshirt covered in neon brush marks.
3. **Marker doodle environment** — large abstract scribbles, floral loops, curved lines, and rough pastel strokes.
4. **Graphic outline treatment** — teal/dark cyan sketch lines around clothing edges and some body contours.
5. **Playful confidence** — relaxed, cool, slightly stylish expression.

---

## Composition Rules
- Vertical 9:16 wallpaper.
- Subject centered and dominant.
- Crop from upper thigh, waist, or chest depending on portrait quality.
- Leave enough colorful background visible around the head and shoulders.
- Pose should feel casual and editorial: hands in pockets, shoulders relaxed, slight lean, chin slightly lifted, soft confident gaze.
- Avoid stiff passport-photo posture.

---

## Face and Identity Rules
The face must remain the most realistic part of the image.

Preserve:
- person’s real facial proportions
- natural skin texture
- eyebrow shape
- eye spacing
- nose bridge and nose shape
- lips and smile logic
- facial hair or clean-shaven look if present
- hair color and haircut logic unless color accents are added

Allowed stylization:
- add subtle neon sketch outline behind hair
- add small painted color streaks around hair edges
- gently improve lighting and editorial sharpness
- slightly adjust expression into relaxed confidence

Do not:
- turn the face into a cartoon
- over-smooth the skin
- change ethnicity or age
- create a celebrity lookalike
- distort face symmetry unnaturally

---

## Outfit Direction
Replace the original clothing with a casual editorial outfit inspired by the reference:

- open short-sleeve shirt, loose overshirt, or light jacket
- simple white tank top or white T-shirt underneath
- beige, tan, or neutral trousers if visible
- optional simple necklace or beads if it fits the person

The outfit should become a canvas for the graphic style:
- neon pink, orange, yellow, coral, cyan paint strokes
- rough marker strokes following fabric folds
- imperfect hand-drawn borders
- teal/dark-cyan sketch outline around collar, pockets, sleeves, and shirt edges

---

## Color Palette
Use a bright pastel-neon palette:

- hot pink
- neon orange
- warm yellow
- coral red
- turquoise/cyan
- lavender
- pale cream
- soft beige
- dark teal sketch outline

The face should keep natural skin colors. The graphic colors should mainly live on:
- shirt
- jacket
- hair-edge accents
- background doodles
- decorative outlines

---

## Background Style
Create a bright studio-like pastel background filled with large abstract hand-drawn marks.

Use:
- oversized flower-like loops
- rough crayon/marker strokes
- scribbles and arcs
- imperfect brush textures
- layered pink/orange/yellow/cyan shapes

Background must feel playful and handmade, not clean vector art.

Avoid:
- realistic rooms
- landscapes
- city backgrounds
- readable words
- logos
- symmetrical patterns

---

## Texture and Rendering
The final image should feel like high-end AI photo editing plus physical mixed-media illustration.

Include:
- realistic skin texture
- visible fabric folds
- paper/grain texture in the colored doodles
- slightly rough marker edges
- layered paint strokes
- uneven handmade linework

Avoid:
- flat vector illustration
- plastic skin
- full cartoon conversion
- overly clean digital gradients
- generic AI fantasy colors

---

## Lighting
Use clean editorial studio lighting:

- soft frontal key light
- gentle face highlights
- mild shadow under chin and jaw
- natural skin contrast
- no dramatic noir lighting
- no harsh colored light on the face

The lighting should support the real portrait while the graphic system creates the energy.

---

```text

Preserve the person’s facial identity, natural skin texture, facial structure, eyes, nose, lips, hair logic, and expression character. Re-stage the subject as a confident editorial portrait with relaxed posture, slightly lifted chin, and casual fashion-shoot energy.

Cover the outfit with rough hand-drawn neon marker strokes in hot pink, orange, yellow, coral, cyan, and lavender. Add dark teal sketch outlines around the collar, shirt edges, sleeves, pockets, and selected body contours.

Keep the face realistic and photographic, with clean studio lighting and light editorial retouching. The surrounding clothes and background should feel graphic, handmade, colorful, energetic, and imperfect.

No text, no logo, no watermark, no readable symbols. Final image must be a polished 9:16 wallpaper.
```

---

## Negative Prompt / Avoid List
Avoid:
- cartoon face
- anime style
- full-body distortion
- celebrity face replacement
- text or typography
- logos or watermark
- realistic graffiti wall
- dull colors
- dark moody lighting
- over-saturated face
- plastic skin
- symmetrical pattern background
- low-resolution doodles
- messy unreadable face

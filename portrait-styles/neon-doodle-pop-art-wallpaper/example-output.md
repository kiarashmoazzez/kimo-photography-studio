# Example Output — Neon Doodle Pop-Art Wallpaper

This example documents the output direction generated from the skill file.

## Example Input
A close-up portrait of a person on a neutral background.

## Example Result Description
A 9:16 vertical phone wallpaper where the person is restaged as a confident editorial portrait. The face remains realistic and recognizable, while the clothing becomes a canvas of neon marker strokes. The background is filled with playful pastel doodles, abstract flower loops, rough crayon marks, and bright hand-drawn brush textures.

## Example Generation Prompt
```text
Transform the uploaded portrait into a 9:16 vertical phone wallpaper in a neon doodle pop-art editorial style.

Preserve the person’s facial identity, natural skin texture, facial structure, eyes, nose, lips, hair logic, and expression character. Re-stage the subject as a confident editorial portrait with relaxed posture, slightly lifted chin, and casual fashion-shoot energy.

Create a realistic sharp face and upper-body portrait. Replace the outfit with a casual open short-sleeve shirt or loose overshirt over a simple white top, with beige/tan trousers if visible. Cover the outfit with rough hand-drawn neon marker strokes in hot pink, orange, yellow, coral, cyan, and lavender. Add dark teal sketch outlines around the collar, shirt edges, sleeves, pockets, and selected body contours.

Create a bright pastel studio background filled with oversized handmade doodles: abstract flower loops, rough crayon marks, scribbles, arcs, and layered neon brush strokes. The background should feel like playful physical marker art, not clean vector graphics.

Keep the face realistic and photographic, with clean studio lighting and light editorial retouching. The surrounding clothes and background should feel graphic, handmade, colorful, energetic, and imperfect.

No text, no logo, no watermark, no readable symbols. Final image must be a polished 9:16 wallpaper.
```

## Visual Result Target
- realistic recognizable face
- casual hands-in-pocket fashion pose
- open painted overshirt
- white base layer
- beige trousers
- neon doodle background
- rough teal graphic outlines
- pastel paper-like texture
